Bridging the Digital Divide

MCS Pages is a nonprofit initiative providing free technology training, compassionate support, and web presence tools to older adults and individuals in crisis. We help them reconnect, rebuild, and thrive in today’s digital world.

Free Tech Help for Real Life

We work with older adults, struggling families, and anyone feeling left behind by today's tech.

How We Can Help

MCS Pages offers completely free digital help for people who need it most. We teach older adults how to use devices to reconnect with family. We help single parents and small business owners launch simple websites. We explain confusing tech in plain English and guide you every step of the way.

Our volunteers and instructors work with patience, compassion, and real-world experience. Whether it’s learning to text, apply for jobs online, use Zoom, or build your online brand — you don’t have to do it alone.
